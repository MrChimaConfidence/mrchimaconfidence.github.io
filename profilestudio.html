Designing a guided experience that helps professionals turn their LinkedIn profiles into real opportunities.

Discipline UX Design
Platform Web
Deliverable End-to-End Flow
Type UX Case Study
01 Overview

The Problem

Professionals who can't communicate their value

Many professionals struggle to present themselves clearly on LinkedIn. Not due to lack of experience, but because they lack clarity on how to structure and communicate their value. This often leads to missed opportunities and low confidence.

The Goal

Remove confusion, build confidence

Design a clear, guided experience that removes confusion, builds user confidence, and encourages users to book a session. Every decision in the product serves this single goal.

The Product

A platform built around clarity

Profile Studio is a web platform that helps professionals improve their LinkedIn presence through guided feedback and structured sessions. Designed to simplify decision-making and move users efficiently from intention to action.

02 Live Prototype

Interactive — Try it yourself

03 Design Approach
01

One decision at a time

The experience breaks complex decisions into simple, progressive steps. Instead of overwhelming users with options, each stage focuses on a single decision, helping users stay focused, confident, and in control.

02

End-to-end flow

The product is structured as a guided journey from defining user goals to completing a booking. Each step builds on the previous one, creating a smooth and predictable experience that never loses the user.

03

Reducing friction at every turn

Every screen was evaluated for unnecessary decisions. Where a choice could be deferred or eliminated, it was. The goal was to ensure users always knew exactly what to do next and why it mattered.

04 Wireframes

Low-fidelity wireframes were created to map out the full user journey and validate the structure before moving into visual design. The focus was on clarity, guiding users step by step, and reducing decision fatigue.

Overview Full-flow wireframe mapping the end-to-end user journey across all screens.
Goal to Direction Breaking the process into guided steps helps users focus on one decision at a time, reducing overwhelm and improving clarity.
Session Booking A simple, linear booking flow reduces friction and makes the final action feel clear and easy to complete.
05 Goal Selection

Users begin by defining their intent

The first screen sets the tone — simple, direct, and focused on what the user actually wants to achieve.

06 Step Progression

Clear stages to reduce overwhelm

The process is broken into clear stages so users always know where they are and what comes next.

07 Booking Flow

From guidance to booked session

Users move seamlessly from guidance to booking a session — the final action feels like a natural conclusion, not an afterthought.

08 Outcome

Clarity that
converts to
bookings

The final experience transforms a complex and often overwhelming process into a clear, structured journey. By reducing friction and guiding users step-by-step, the design improves clarity, builds confidence, and increases the likelihood of session bookings.
