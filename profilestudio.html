01 Overview

The Problem

Professionals who struggle to present their value

Many professionals have strong experience but struggle to communicate it clearly on LinkedIn. The issue isn't always capability. It's often uncertainty around what to say, what to prioritize, and how to structure their profile in a way that feels intentional. As a result, opportunities are missed before conversations even begin.

The Goal

Create clarity through guided progression

Design a focused experience that removes ambiguity and helps users move confidently from intention to action. Every interaction was designed to simplify decision-making and make the process feel manageable from start to finish.

The Product

A platform designed around direction

Profile Studio is a guided web experience that helps professionals improve their LinkedIn presence through structured recommendations and personalized sessions. The platform breaks the process into clear stages, helping users move from uncertainty to a concrete improvement plan.

03 Design Approach
01

One focused decision at a time

Complex processes become easier when users are only asked to make one meaningful choice at each stage. The experience was intentionally structured to reduce cognitive load and keep users moving forward without hesitation.

02

A continuous end-to-end flow

Each screen builds directly on the previous one, creating a predictable and uninterrupted journey. Instead of isolated interactions, the product behaves as a connected flow with a clear sense of progression.

03

Reducing friction through structure

Every interaction was evaluated for unnecessary complexity. Actions that could be simplified, delayed, or removed were intentionally minimized so users could stay focused on completing their goal rather than navigating the interface itself.

04 Wireframes

Low-fidelity wireframes were used to map the complete user journey before visual design began. The focus at this stage was structure. Understanding how users would move through the experience, where decisions occurred, and how progression could remain intuitive throughout the flow.

Overview Early wireframe explorations focused on establishing the full journey from onboarding to booking. The priority was not visual polish but ensuring the experience felt guided, sequential, and easy to follow.
Goal to Direction Breaking the experience into smaller stages helped reduce overwhelm and gave users a clearer sense of progress. Each step introduced only the information necessary for the next decision.
Session Booking The booking experience was designed to feel lightweight and direct. By the time users reached scheduling, most major decisions had already been resolved earlier in the flow.
05 Goal Selection

Users begin by defining their intent

The experience starts by understanding what the user is actually trying to achieve. Whether the goal is landing a job, attracting recruiters, or improving visibility, the platform adapts recommendations based on that initial direction. This creates a more personalized experience while reducing unnecessary options later in the journey.

06 Step Progression

Clear stages that maintain momentum

The process is divided into visible stages so users always understand where they are, what they've completed, and what comes next. This structure creates reassurance throughout the experience and prevents users from feeling lost inside the flow.

07 Booking Flow

From recommendation to confirmed session

Once users complete the guided flow, the platform translates their inputs into a tailored recommendation and booking experience. The transition into scheduling feels like a natural continuation of the journey rather than a separate task. The final flow was designed to feel simple, calm, and intentional from selection to confirmation.

08 Outcome

Designing
clarity into
the experience

The final product transforms what can often feel like an overwhelming process into a guided and structured journey. By simplifying decisions and maintaining progression throughout the experience, the design encourages confidence, direction, and action.

Rather than asking users to figure everything out on their own, the platform provides a clearer path forward, one step at a time.
