Vanguard Gallery

Mobile UX Case Study

A seamless mobile experience for discovering artworks and connecting with artists.

Key screens from the Vanguard Gallery mobile experience

Overview

Vanguard Gallery is a mobile app concept designed to deepen the connection between users and artists by enabling intuitive browsing and exploration.

Problem

Users can view artworks but often lack context about the artist and struggle to explore further works, creating a disconnected experience.

Goal

Design a seamless discovery experience that connects artwork, artist information, and further exploration.

Key Insight

Users don’t just want to view art — they want context and continuity.

Design Decisions

1. Continuous exploration flow
Connected artwork, artist, and related works to reduce drop-off.

2. Clear next steps
Added strong actions to guide users forward.

3. Improved interaction clarity
Enhanced visual cues to make interactions obvious.

Outcome

The final design creates a more intuitive and connected browsing experience, allowing users to move seamlessly from viewing artwork to deeper exploration.
