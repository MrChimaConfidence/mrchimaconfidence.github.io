Overview

Vanguard Gallery is a mobile app concept designed to create a calm, immersive experience while making interactions feel intentional and responsive.

Problem

Browsing artworks often feels passive. Users can scroll endlessly, but there’s little guidance, feedback, or connection to what they’re seeing.

Key Insight

Users weren’t struggling to use the app,
they were struggling to feel guided.

Experience Flow

Discovery

Users explore artworks but lack clear direction forward.

Deep Exploration

Users want to move from artwork to artist and continue exploring seamlessly.

Wireframing & Exploration

To translate insights into structure, I created low-fidelity wireframes focused on clarity, hierarchy, and navigation flow.

The goal was to reduce cognitive load and ensure users could seamlessly move between artworks and artist profiles without losing context.

These explorations helped define layout patterns and validate interaction decisions before moving into visual design.

Goal

Design a guided, responsive experience that transforms passive browsing into active exploration.

Pain Points

Information Overload

Too many artworks without structure.

Dead-Ends

No clear way forward.

Hesitation

Users unsure of next steps.

Unclear UI

Interactions not obvious.

Iteration

Early layouts revealed that too many elements competing for attention reduced clarity.

I refined the structure by simplifying content hierarchy, improving spacing, and making key actions more prominent.

This ensured users always had a clear sense of direction while exploring.

Design Decisions

Continuous exploration flow, clear next steps, and improved interaction clarity.

Interaction Design

Subtle interactions were introduced to make the interface feel responsive and alive. Artwork cards scale on hover, buttons provide feedback, and transitions connect screens smoothly.

Interaction Feedback

Artwork cards respond to user interaction with subtle scaling, creating immediate visual feedback and a more responsive feel.

Smooth Transitions

Screen transitions were designed to feel continuous and fluid.

Final Screens

Outcome

The final experience feels more guided, responsive, and intentional. Users are no longer just browsing, they are actively exploring.
