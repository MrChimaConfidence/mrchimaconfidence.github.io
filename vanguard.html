Vanguard Gallery

Mobile UX Case Study

Designing a seamless way to discover not just art, but the artists behind it.

A preview of the Vanguard Gallery experience

ROLE

UX Designer

DURATION

4 Weeks

TOOLS

Figma · Google Forms · Slides

Overview

Vanguard Gallery is a mobile concept designed to bridge the gap between artwork and artist discovery.

The goal was to create a seamless Artist Discovery Loop that keeps users engaged as they move from artwork to artist and deeper exploration.

Problem

Users can view artworks but often lack context about the artist.

This creates a disconnected experience where users struggle to explore further.

Goal

Design a seamless discovery experience connecting artwork, artist information, and exploration.

User Research

A usability study with five participants revealed hesitation at key navigation points.

Key Insight

Users were not struggling to complete tasks — they were struggling to understand what to do next.

Pain Points

1. Information overload

2. Navigation dead-ends

3. Hesitation in actions

4. Unclear interactions

Design Decisions

1. Continuous exploration flow

2. Clear next steps

3. Better interaction clarity

Final Screens

Outcome

A smoother, more intuitive experience that keeps users engaged and exploring.
