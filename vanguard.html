Vanguard Gallery

Mobile UX Case Study

A seamless mobile experience for discovering artworks and connecting with artists.

A preview of the Vanguard Gallery experience

Overview

Vanguard Gallery is a mobile app concept designed to deepen the connection between users and artists by enabling intuitive browsing and exploration.

Problem

Users can view artworks but often lack context about the artist and struggle to explore further works.

Goal

Design a seamless discovery experience connecting artwork, artist, and exploration.

Key Insight

Users don’t just want to view art — they want context and continuity.

Design Decisions

Continuous exploration flow
Connected artwork, artist, and related works.

Clear next steps
Guided users forward with strong actions.

Improved interaction clarity
Enhanced visual cues for usability.

Final Screens

Key moments from the Vanguard Gallery experience

Outcome

The final design enables a seamless transition from viewing artwork to deeper exploration.
