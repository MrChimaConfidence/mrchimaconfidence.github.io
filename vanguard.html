Vanguard Gallery

Mobile UX Case Study

Designing a seamless way to discover not just art, but the artists behind it.

A preview of the Vanguard Gallery experience

ROLE

UX Designer

DURATION

4 Weeks

TOOLS

Figma · Google Forms · Slides

Overview

Vanguard Gallery is a mobile concept designed to bridge the gap between artwork and artist discovery.

Problem

Users can view artworks but often lack context about the artist, creating a disconnected experience.

Key Insight

Users were not struggling to complete tasks — they were struggling to understand what to do next.

Pain Points

Information Overload Too many artworks without structure.
Dead-Ends No clear way to continue browsing.
Hesitation Users unsure of next actions.
Unclear UI Interactive elements not obvious.

Design Decisions

Continuous exploration flow, clear next steps, and improved interaction clarity.

Final Screens

Outcome

A smoother, more intuitive experience that keeps users engaged and exploring.
