Vanguard Gallery

Mobile UX Case Study

Overview

A seamless mobile experience for discovering artworks and connecting with artists.

Problem

Users can view artworks but often lack context about the artist, creating a disconnected experience.

Goal

Design a seamless discovery experience connecting artwork, artist, and exploration.

Key Insight

Users could navigate the app.
But they didn’t always know what to do next.

Pain Points

Too many artworks without structure
No clear next steps
Users hesitate
Unclear interactions

Experience Flow

Discovery

Users explore artworks but lack clear direction forward.

Deep Exploration

Users want to move from artwork to artist seamlessly.

Final Screens

Outcome

A more intuitive and connected browsing experience that enables continuous exploration.
