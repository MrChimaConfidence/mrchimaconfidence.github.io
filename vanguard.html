Vanguard Gallery

Mobile UX Case Study

Designing a seamless way to discover not just art, but the artists behind it.

Key Insight

Users could navigate the app.
But they didn’t always know what to do next.

Pain Points

Information Overload

Too many artworks without structure.

Dead-Ends

No clear way to continue browsing.

Hesitation

Users unsure of next actions.

Unclear UI

Interactive elements not obvious.
