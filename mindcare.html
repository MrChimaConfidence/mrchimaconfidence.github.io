MindCare

Emotional Check-in Experience

A guided mental health experience designed to help users reflect on their emotions and access support without feeling overwhelmed.

View Prototype →

Overview

MindCare transforms emotional reflection into a simple, structured flow. Instead of overwhelming users with too many options, the experience guides them step-by-step to understand how they feel and what to do next.

The Problem

Many mental health apps overwhelm users with too many choices at the wrong moment, making it harder to take the first step.

The Solution

A step-by-step emotional check-in that reduces friction, uses supportive language, and guides users toward the right action based on how they feel.

Wireframing & Flow Exploration

Low-fidelity wireframes were used to explore the structure of the emotional check-in flow. The focus was on reducing cognitive load and guiding users step-by-step before moving into visual design.

Interaction Design

Simple tap-based interactions provide immediate feedback, helping users feel confident in their selections without confusion.

Emotional Insight

The system reflects user input using calm, supportive language, helping users feel understood without judgment or pressure.

Support Options

Support is presented as clear, structured options with a recommended path to reduce decision fatigue.

Key Design Decisions

  • Designed for mobile-first interaction (no hover states)
  • Clear tap feedback and visible state changes
  • Structured flow to reduce cognitive overload
  • Supportive, non-judgmental language
  • Guided decision-making through recommendations

Outcome

The final experience reduces cognitive load and helps users take meaningful action faster during emotional distress, creating a calmer and more supportive journey.
