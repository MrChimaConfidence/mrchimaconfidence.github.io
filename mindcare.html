MindCare

Emotional Check-in Experience

A guided experience designed to help users reflect on their emotions and access support in a calm, non-overwhelming way.

View Prototype →

Overview

MindCare simplifies emotional reflection into a clear, structured flow that helps users understand how they feel and what to do next.

The Problem

Many mental health tools feel overwhelming or too complex, making it difficult for users to take the first step.

The Solution

A step-by-step check-in that reduces friction and provides clarity through simple interactions and supportive language.

User Flow

Interaction Design

Tap-based interactions provide immediate feedback, helping users clearly understand their selections.

Emotional Insight

The experience uses neutral, supportive language to acknowledge emotions without overwhelming the user.

Support Options

Users are guided toward helpful actions through a structured set of options, with a recommended path to reduce decision fatigue.

Key Design Decisions

  • Designed for mobile interaction (no hover)
  • Clear tap feedback and state changes
  • Simple, structured flow to reduce overwhelm
  • Supportive and non-assumptive language
  • Guided decision-making through recommendation
